This document defines the complete **UI/UX architecture** for a production-ready AI chatbot SaaS platform. The system enables businesses to build, deploy, monitor, and optimize AI-powered sales and support bots.

The design follows principles used by platforms like **Intercom, Drift, ManyChat, Ada, and HubSpot**.

---

- Manage leads

# **2. Core Product Use Cases**

## **Create Chatbot**

Workflow:

1. Click “Create Bot”
2. Select template
3. Enter business information
4. Upload knowledge base
5. Configure conversation logic
6. Connect integrations
7. Test bot
8. Deploy bot

---

## **Configure Conversation Logic**

Workflow:

1. Open Bot Builder
2. Edit conversation stages
3. Define tasks and actions
4. Save configuration
5. Test conversation simulation

---

## **Upload Knowledge Base**

Workflow:

1. Navigate to Knowledge Base
2. Add articles or documents
3. Upload files or links
4. Train AI model

---

## **Monitor Conversations**

Workflow:

1. Open Conversations dashboard
2. Select conversation
3. View timeline
4. Take manual control if needed

---

## **Analyze Analytics**

Workflow:

1. Open Analytics dashboard
2. Select time range
3. View metrics and charts
4. Identify optimization opportunities

---

## **Manage Leads**

Workflow:

1. View leads generated by chatbot
2. Access lead profiles
3. Export or sync with CRM
